Return and Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

At ElectroWell, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not satisfied, we're here to help with returns and refunds.

1. Return Period

1.1 Standard Return Window

  • 30 days from date of delivery for most products
  • Extended return periods may apply during holiday seasons
  • Returns must be initiated within the return window

1.2 Special Products

  • Custom-configured items: Non-returnable (unless defective)
  • Special orders: Subject to restocking fee
  • Clearance items: Final sale (as marked)

2. Return Eligibility

2.1 Returnable Items

Items must meet ALL of the following conditions:

  • ✅ Within 30-day return window
  • ✅ Unused and in original condition
  • ✅ Original packaging intact
  • ✅ All accessories and documentation included
  • ✅ No physical damage or modifications
  • ✅ Return authorization (RMA) obtained

2.2 Non-Returnable Items

  • ❌ Custom-configured or programmed PLCs/drives
  • ❌ Items marked as "non-returnable" or "final sale"
  • ❌ Opened software or downloadable products
  • ❌ Items without original packaging
  • ❌ Items damaged by customer use or mishandling
  • ❌ Items purchased more than 30 days ago

3. Return Process

3.1 Step 1: Request RMA (Return Authorization)

Before returning any item, you must:

  1. Contact us at support@electrowell.com or [Phone Number]
  2. Provide order number and reason for return
  3. Wait for RMA number (provided within 1-2 business days)

Important: Returns without RMA number will be refused.

3.2 Step 2: Pack Your Return

  • Use original packaging if possible
  • Include all accessories, manuals, and documentation
  • Clearly write RMA number on outside of package
  • Secure packaging to prevent damage during transit

3.3 Step 3: Ship Your Return

  • Ship to address provided in RMA email
  • Use trackable shipping method
  • Keep shipping receipt and tracking number
  • Package and ship within 7 days of receiving RMA

4. Return Shipping Costs

4.1 Customer Responsibility

Customer pays return shipping unless:

  • Item is defective or damaged
  • Wrong item was shipped
  • Item doesn't match description

4.2 Our Responsibility

We pay return shipping for:

  • Defective products
  • Shipping errors
  • Products not as described

4.3 Shipping Insurance

We recommend insuring high-value returns. ElectroWell is not responsible for items lost or damaged during return shipping.

5. Refund Process

5.1 Inspection Period

  • Returns are inspected within 2-3 business days of receipt
  • You will be notified of inspection results
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after approval

5.2 Refund Method

  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Credit card refunds: 5-10 business days to appear
  • PayPal refunds: 1-3 business days

5.3 Refund Amount

You will receive:

  • ✅ Full product price (if approved)
  • ✅ Original shipping costs (if our error)
  • ❌ Return shipping costs (unless our error)
  • ❌ Restocking fee may apply (see below)

6. Restocking Fees

6.1 When Applicable

Restocking fees may apply to:

  • Special order items: Up to 25%
  • High-value industrial equipment: Up to 15%
  • Items returned after 14 days: Up to 10%

6.2 No Restocking Fee

No fee for:

  • Defective products
  • Our shipping errors
  • Products not as described

7. Exchanges

7.1 Product Exchanges

  • We gladly exchange defective or damaged items
  • Exchanges for different products require return and new purchase
  • Price differences may apply

7.2 Exchange Process

  1. Follow return process above
  2. Indicate desired exchange in RMA request
  3. Replacement ships when return is received and approved

8. Defective or Damaged Products

8.1 Reporting Damage

  • Report defects or damage within 48 hours of delivery
  • Take photos of damage (package and product)
  • Keep all packaging materials
  • Contact us immediately at support@electrowell.com

8.2 Resolution Options

We offer:

  • Full refund
  • Replacement product
  • Repair (if applicable)

8.3 Shipping Damage

  • DO NOT accept severely damaged packages
  • Note damage on delivery receipt
  • File claim with carrier if damage discovered later

9. Warranty Returns

9.1 Manufacturer Warranties

Products are covered by manufacturer warranties. Warranty periods vary by product:

  • Most PLCs and drives: 1-2 years
  • Refer to product documentation for specific terms
  • Some warranties require product registration

9.2 Warranty Claims

  • Contact us to initiate warranty claim
  • Proof of purchase required
  • Follow manufacturer warranty process
  • We facilitate warranty repairs and replacements

9.3 Warranty Exclusions

Warranties do NOT cover:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Misuse or abuse
  • Improper installation
  • Unauthorized modifications
  • Acts of nature (lightning, flood, etc.)

10. Cancellations

10.1 Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled before shipping
  • Contact us immediately at support@electrowell.com
  • Once shipped, follow return process

10.2 Cancellation Fees

  • No fee if cancelled before shipping
  • Special orders may incur fees if already processed
  • Custom configurations are non-cancellable

11. International Returns

11.1 Return Shipping

  • Customer responsible for return shipping costs
  • Use trackable international shipping method
  • Declare accurate value on customs forms

11.2 Customs and Duties

  • ElectroWell not responsible for import duties on returns
  • Original customs fees non-refundable
  • Returns may be subject to customs inspection

12. Refused or Undeliverable Returns

Returns will be refused if:

  • No RMA number on package
  • Returned after 30-day window
  • Product not eligible for return
  • Package damaged or improperly packed

13. Australian Consumer Law

For Australian customers:

  • Our goods come with guarantees under Australian Consumer Law
  • You are entitled to a replacement or refund for a major failure
  • You are entitled to compensation for reasonably foreseeable loss or damage
  • Goods must be of acceptable quality and fit for purpose

These rights apply in addition to our return policy and cannot be excluded.
