HVAC Applications & Energy Savings

Chiller Plant Optimization

VFD control of chillers, condenser pumps, and cooling towers for maximum efficiency:

  • Energy Savings: 30-50% reduction in chiller plant energy consumption
  • Sequencing Logic: Optimal chiller staging based on load conditions
  • Condenser Water Reset: Automatically adjust condenser water temperature
  • Free Cooling: Economizer control when outdoor temps allow

Recommended Products: Danfoss FC 102 drives with cascade PID, Schneider PowerLogic meters

Air Handling Unit (AHU) Control

Complete AHU automation including supply/return fans, mixed air dampers, and heating/cooling coils:

  • VAV Systems: Variable air volume control with static pressure reset
  • Economizer Control: Maximize free cooling with outdoor air
  • Demand Control Ventilation: CO2-based outdoor air control
  • Filter Monitoring: Pressure differential sensors for filter change alerts

Recommended Products: Siemens Desigo controllers with BACnet/IP, Belimo actuators

Boiler & Heating Systems

Efficient control of boilers, pumps, and distribution systems:

  • Lead/Lag Control: Automatic boiler staging based on demand
  • Outdoor Air Reset: Adjust hot water temperature based on outdoor conditions
  • Variable Speed Pumping: VFD control of primary/secondary loops
  • Burner Management: Safe startup and shutdown sequences

Recommended Products: Allen-Bradley CompactLogix, Danfoss VLT drives, Honeywell flame safeguard

Communication Protocols for Building Automation

ElectroWell products support all major building automation protocols:

Open Protocols

  • BACnet/IP: Industry-standard protocol for BMS
  • BACnet MS/TP: RS-485 fieldbus for controllers
  • Modbus TCP/RTU: Widely supported for integration
  • LonWorks: Legacy building control networks
  • KNX: European standard for building automation

HVAC-Specific Protocols

  • DALI: Digital Addressable Lighting Interface
  • BACNET/MSTP: Master-Slave/Token-Passing
  • Tridium Niagara: Open framework for integration
  • OPC UA: Industrial connectivity to BMS
