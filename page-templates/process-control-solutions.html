Process Safety & Compliance

ElectroWell provides safety-rated components for critical process applications:

Safety Instrumented Systems (SIS)

  • SIL Rated PLCs: Allen-Bradley GuardLogix, Siemens S7-1500F (up to SIL 3/PLe)
  • Emergency Shutdown: Redundant safety I/O, safety relays, E-stop circuits
  • Burner Management: Flame monitoring, gas train control, purge sequences
  • High Integrity Pressure Protection (HIPPS): Rapid pressure relief systems

Regulatory Compliance

  • IEC 61508: Functional safety standard for safety instrumented systems
  • IEC 61511: Process industry safety lifecycle (SIL verification)
  • ISA-88: Batch control standards for recipe management
  • ISA-95: Enterprise-control system integration
  • ATEX/IECEx: Explosion protection for hazardous areas
  • FDA 21 CFR Part 11: Electronic records and signatures (pharmaceutical)
