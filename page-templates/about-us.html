Who We Are

ElectroWell is a leading supplier of industrial automation products, specializing in PLCs (Programmable Logic Controllers), VFDs (Variable Frequency Drives), HMIs, and industrial control systems. We provide businesses and professionals worldwide with high-quality automation components from the industry's most trusted manufacturers.

Our Mission

We're committed to being your dedicated resource for industrial automation. Our mission is to provide:

  • Quality Products: Genuine products from leading manufacturers including Omron, Danfoss, Vacon, Allen-Bradley, Schneider Electric, and Emerson
  • Expert Support: Technical expertise to help you select the right components for your application
  • Global Delivery: Worldwide shipping to get products to you quickly and safely
  • Competitive Pricing: Fair pricing on industrial automation equipment
